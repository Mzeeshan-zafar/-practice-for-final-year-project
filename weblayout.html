🌍 1. Know What WHO Actually Does

  • WHO is a UN agency that works for global public health.

  • It:Fights diseases (like COVID-19, polio, malaria, TB)

  • Sets health standards and guidelines

  • Helps countries improve their health systems

  • Responds to health emergencies (outbreaks, disasters, etc.)
